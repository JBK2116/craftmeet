Craftmeet

Structured, idea-driven meetings — in real time.

Craftmeet is a real-time meeting platform for hosting structured, idea-driven sessions. Build meetings with flexible question formats — multiple choice, long answer, ranked voting, rating scales, and yes/no polls. Launch them live with your group, watch responses stream in, and receive an AI-generated PDF summary the moment your meeting ends.

How Craftmeet uses Google data

Craftmeet uses Sign in with Google solely for account creation and authentication. When you sign in, we request the following scopes:

  • openid — to verify your Google identity and associate it with your Craftmeet account.
  • profile — to retrieve your display name and profile picture for your account.
  • email — to retrieve your email address for account identification, communication, and support.

We use this information exclusively to provision and secure your Craftmeet account. We do not access your Google Drive, Calendar, Contacts, or any other Google service. We do not share your Google account data with third parties. You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account permissions page.
